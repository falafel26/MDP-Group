Building Foundations of Trust and Excellence

MDP Infra (India) Pvt. Ltd. has been delivering professional civil and infrastructure solutions since 2003. As the construction and infrastructure arm of MDP Group, we are recognized as a trusted partner for government agencies, institutional clients, and private enterprises across India.

With over two decades of experience, MDP Infra delivers high-quality, sustainable, and innovative civil and infrastructure solutions — completed on time, within budget, and to the highest engineering standards. Backed by the strength and credibility of MDP Group, we execute projects with a strong focus on durability, precision, safety, and disciplined project management. From groundwork to final structural delivery, every project is built on a foundation of trust and technical excellence. Our services include the following key areas:

Impact

Our Services

Civil Construction Works

We execute institutional buildings, housing projects, industrial facilities, and large-scale public infrastructure developments. Our expertise includes foundation engineering, RCC structures, masonry, finishing works, and comprehensive external development.

Structural & Concrete Solutions

Our structural capabilities include reinforced concrete construction, structural design execution, slab systems, large-span frameworks, and high-performance load-bearing systems with strict quality and safety standards.

Infrastructure Development

We undertake complete infrastructure development including water supply systems, drainage, stormwater networks, external development works, firefighting systems, and electrical integration.

Electrical & MEP Integration

We provide integrated electrical works including HVAC systems, firefighting installations, lighting, power distribution, compact substations, DG sets, and compliant safety systems.

Site Development & Preparation

Our services include land clearing, grading, soil compaction, surveying, layout marking, and access road development to ensure a stable and well-prepared foundation for construction.

EPC Solutions – End-to-End Project Delivery

MDP Infra delivers integrated EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) solutions, providing clients with a single-point responsibility model for infrastructure and civil construction projects.

Our EPC approach ensures seamless coordination across design, sourcing, and execution — enabling faster delivery, cost optimization, and uncompromised quality.

Engineering

Our engineering division provides detailed planning, structural design, and technical analysis tailored to project requirements. From feasibility studies to execution drawings, we ensure safety, efficiency, and long-term performance.

Procurement

We manage strategic sourcing, vendor coordination, and supply chain operations to ensure timely availability of quality materials, machinery, and project resources while maintaining cost efficiency.

Construction

Our construction team executes civil and infrastructure projects with precision, safety, and disciplined management — ensuring timely delivery and uncompromised structural quality.
