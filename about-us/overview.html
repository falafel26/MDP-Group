About Us

MDP Group is a diversified business group delivering infrastructure, technology, manufacturing, and engineering solutions across India. Founded in 2003, the group has evolved into a trusted partner for government institutions, enterprises, and public infrastructure projects.

With expertise spanning civil construction, electrical distribution projects, IT infrastructure, software development, and manufacturing, MDP Group combines engineering excellence with technological innovation to deliver integrated solutions.

Over the years, the group has successfully executed large-scale projects including government buildings, institutional campuses, smart classrooms, data centers, electrical infrastructure networks, and advanced digital solutions.

Driven by a commitment to quality, safety, and sustainability, MDP Group focuses on delivering projects on time, within budget, and with uncompromising standards of excellence. Today, the group continues to expand its footprint across infrastructure, technology, and industrial sectors while contributing to national development.
