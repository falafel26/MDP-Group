Group Companies

MDP Group operates through multiple specialized companies that together deliver integrated infrastructure and technology solutions.

MDP Infra (India) Pvt. Ltd.

The flagship company of the group specializing in civil construction, infrastructure development, and integrated project execution. The company has successfully delivered major institutional, residential, and government projects across India.

MDP Powertech Pvt. Ltd.

Operates manufacturing facilities producing distribution transformers, prestressed concrete poles, electrical structures, and other infrastructure components required for power and civil projects.

Through these companies, MDP Group delivers complete end-to-end solutions from manufacturing and engineering to technology implementation and infrastructure development.

MDP Infotech Pvt. Ltd.

Provides enterprise IT infrastructure, system integration, software development, cybersecurity solutions, and data center technologies for government and enterprise clients.
