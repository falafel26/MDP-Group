Director’s Message

Our journey is built on years of hard work, trust, and a deep respect for engineering excellence. What began as a vision to create reliable power solutions has grown into a manufacturing legacy driven by quality and integrity.

Every transformer we manufacture reflects our commitment to durability, safety, and long-term performance. We believe trust is earned over time, through consistent delivery, honest practices, and standing by our customers in every condition.

As we move forward, we remain rooted in our values while embracing innovation, determined to contribute meaningfully to India’s power infrastructure for generations to come.

Yogesh Parashar, Director & CEO
